Resources for Students & Young Scholars

A curated guide of opportunities for students and early-career scholars in public policy and administration: journals, associations, conferences, awards, funding, methods training, job-market advice, and teaching resources.

This is a living resource developed together with Dr. Hannah Lebovits, with valuable contributions from many colleagues. It is not exhaustive, and we warmly welcome suggestions and feedback. See the full living document (also shared here).

Using AI Responsibly in Research
  • COPE: Authorship and AI Tools – AI cannot be an author; disclose how it was used
  • ICMJE Recommendations – disclosing AI-assisted technologies in manuscripts
  • UNESCO: Guidance for Generative AI in Education and Research
  • Publisher AI policies, compared (library guide), and Elsevier's author policy as an example

Always check each journal's author guidelines before submitting – AI-disclosure requirements vary by publisher.
